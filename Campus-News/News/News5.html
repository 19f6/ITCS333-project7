UOB Concludes COBS Ramadan Soccer Tournament With Great Enthusiasm

The University of Bahrain has successfully concluded the COBS Ramadan Soccer Tournament, with the Milies Galali team emerging as champions. Around 100 students participated in the event, organized by the College of Business Administration Society under the Deanship of Student Affairs. The Cyber FC team secured second place.
