Career Success Session for IT Students

The Department of Information Systems, College of Information Technology, hosted a student- alumni session to encourage discussions on career paths, professional relationships, and future goals.

Two alumni, Ahmed Ramadan, Director of Solution Development and Management at BNET, and Salman Rahimi, Network and Delivery Services Specialist at NBB, shared their journeys to professional success. The session was well received by students, who actively asked questions and exchanged ideas.
